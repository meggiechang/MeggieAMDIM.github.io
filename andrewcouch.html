About

Contributions to AMDIM

I apply analytical and statistical methods to better understand the challenging nature of decision-making and business models for Small and Medium-Sized Enterprises (SMEs) in their pursuit of Additive Manufacturing (AM) technologies. The early stages of my work focuses on conducting a broad assessment of published research in order to identify and extract the underlying strategies that are most conducive to success given the limitations imposed on SMEs. In turn, this work helps SMEs to better navigate the complex world of AM through enhancements in decision-making, strategy, and operations management. My findings help to guide SMEs towards AM technology adoption as they pursue the narrow pathway to success.
